Why is NVIDIA important?

NVIDIA is a company that has advanced computer technology greatly. They are most well known for their powerful graphics cards and advancements in Artificial Intelligence The company was founded in 1993 by Jen-Hsun "Jensen" Huang, Curtis Priem, and Chris Malachowsky and is headquartered in Santa Clara, Calif.
